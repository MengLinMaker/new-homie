Suburb Information

Dandenong, VIC 3175

Part of: Greater Dandenong Council No data available

The size of Dandenong is approximately 11.3 square kilometres. It has 22 parks covering nearly 16.6% of total area. The population of Dandenong in 2011 was 24,919 people. By 2016 the population was 29,910 showing a population growth of 20.0% in the area during that time. The predominant age group in Dandenong is 20-29 years. Households in Dandenong are primarily couples with children and are likely to be repaying $1400 - $1799 per month on mortgage repayments. In general, people in Dandenong work in a trades occupation. In 2011, 42.8% of the homes in Dandenong were owner-occupied compared with 39.7% in 2016. (source: Australian Bureau of Statistics)
